Afternoon Dives

Aloha! We specialize in small groups and personalized attention. All of our dives are specifically tailored to our divers. These dives are tailored more for those beginner divers and for those without their Scuba Certification. That does not mean that these dives are less fun or you won't see as much, it just means we wont be going as deep.

Dive FAQs

Dive Requirements

No experience in diving is necessary. These dives are not very advanced, but are just as fun and interesting as our advanced dives.

Dive Locations

  • Turtle Canyons

    The max depth at this site is 35 feet. This is the premiere spot for turtles. This is a calm site perfect for beginners even the most experienced divers will come up amazed.

  • Nautilus Reef

    Nautilus Reef is enjoyable for beginners and experienced divers with a maximum depth of 40 feet. This is also one of the only places where large schools of Mu can be found.

How to Book With Us

All of our dives are specifically tailored to our divers, so we like to have a consultation with you over email or phone in order to better understand your wants and needs so we can give you the best diving experience on Waikiki.
